Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salami
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Artichoke Hearts
  • Spinach
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Pizza

Description

Pizza is an all american classic rooted from Italy. This my italian/greek spin on it with kalamata olives, artichoke hearts, salami, and spinach. With a garlic olive oil crust.

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

    2. Dough:

  2. Remove dough from bag and let it sit for 30min.
  3. Shape dough into a flat rectangle (using flour ass needed)

    4. Toppings

  4. Apply an even coat of tomato sauce.
  5. Add cheese (to your liking).
  6. Add the salami, olives, and artichokes.

    7. Crust:

  7. Mince the garlic.
  8. In a bowl, mix together the minced garlic, olive oil, and salt
  9. Use a brush and apply mixture to crust.

    10. Bake:

  10. Bake in oven for 10min.
  11. Remove from oven and add spinach.
  12. Bake in oven for 10min.

Viola! Delicious Pizza!
